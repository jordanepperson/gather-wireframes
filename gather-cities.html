The Model

A Gather Cities event is led by your church.

Your church runs 80 to 90 percent of a Gather Cities event: the space, the people, the promotion, the pastoral leadership. Gather provides the rest — playbooks, promotional assets, and a point of contact who knows the format. We don't run the event for you, and there's no lighter, one-off version to choose instead. It's one model, so every city knows exactly what it's signing up for.

Why It Matters

This is momentum, not a finish line.

Bringing Gather to your city connects your congregation to a global rhythm of prayer, worship, and mobilization already moving through homes and churches everywhere. It's the on-ramp toward hosting Gather27 in 2027, not a one-off event that stands on its own. Every city that gathers now is a city ready to gather the world when Gather27 arrives.

FAQ

What's the timeline?

Gather Cities is building toward 1,000 activated cities by 2030. We're just getting started.

What does my church commit to?

Leading 80 to 90 percent of the event — space, people, and promotion — with Gather providing playbooks and light support.

Is there a cost?

No fee to partner. Your church covers its own local event costs.

How is this different from hosting Gather27?

Gather Cities events happen locally, on your own timeline, building momentum in your city right now. Gather27 is the global broadcast in 2027 that every Gather Cities activation is building toward — many of our first hosts plan to host Gather27 watch parties too.
