What It Is

Your church, leading your city.

Gather Cities is a night your church leads for your city: worship, prayer, and a shared hope for what God is doing right where you are. Your church brings the people and the platform. We bring the playbook, the promotional assets, and a real person to call with questions.

The first cities are already stepping up, months ahead of Gather27, because momentum like this doesn't wait for a global broadcast to start it.

Why It Matters

This is how we build momentum

FAQ

What is Gather Cities?

A local night of worship, prayer, and vision for your city, led by your own church. It's part of a bigger movement building toward 1,000 activated cities by 2030.

How do churches play a role?

Your church leads 80 to 90 percent of the night: the space, the people, the promotion, the pastor on the stage. Gather provides the playbook, promotional assets, and a real person to call with questions.

Is there a cost?

No fee to partner. Your church covers its own local event costs.

How is this different than Gather27?

Gather Cities happens locally, on your own timeline, building momentum in your city right now. Gather27 is the global broadcast in 2027 that every Gather Cities activation is building toward.
