One City at a Time

This is just the beginning

When local pastors lead their churches to gather their city, something happens that outlasts a single night: people reconnect to a local church, congregations find fresh unity, and a city gets to see what happens when the Church shows up together. If revival is already happening where you are, we'd love to help you gather your city around it.

Let's Be Clear

What Gather Cities is not

Pastors have seen plenty of programs come and go. Before you say yes, here's what Gather Cities won't ask you to be.

  • × A new church
  • × A Jennie Allen tour stop
  • × A watch party
  • × An event dropped into your city from the outside
  • × An ongoing ministry that competes with local churches

It's a night your own church leads, for people you already know how to reach.

FAQ

What is Gather Cities?

A local night of worship, prayer, and vision for your city, led by your own church. It's part of a bigger movement building toward 1,000 activated cities by 2030.

How do churches play a role?

Your church leads 80 to 90 percent of the night: the space, the people, the promotion, the pastor on the stage. Gather provides the playbook, promotional assets, and a real person to call with questions.

Is there a cost?

No fee to partner. Your church covers its own local event costs.

How is this different than Gather27?

Gather Cities is its own thing: a night your own church leads for your city, on your own timeline. Gather27 is a separate global broadcast in 2027. Some cities that gather now may also take part in Gather27 later, but Gather Cities isn't a lead-up event or a promotional stop for it.
