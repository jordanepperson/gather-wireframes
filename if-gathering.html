A Gathering for Women

Making disciples everywhere

IF gathers women to encourage one another toward courageous obedience to Jesus. Obedience is personal, but courage grows in community. We come together to pray, worship, learn from Scripture, and encourage one another through honest conversations about faith and life, becoming the everyday rhythm that carries IF:Gathering forward all year long.

FAQ

Is IF:Gathering free?

Yes. The broadcast is completely free to watch, wherever you are.

How do I get tickets to the main IF:Gathering?

There's no traditional ticket to buy — IF:Gathering is built to be experienced with people, not alone. Gather your own IF:Local or find one meeting near you, and watch together right where you are. If you'd rather be in the room, we'll share details on attending in person in Dallas as the date gets closer.

Why is IF:Gathering two days again?

We're returning to the original two-day format for 2027, after 2026's one-night event, IF:One Night — more room for teaching, worship, and community.

What is an IF:Local?

A small group — as few as 5 people — gathering year-round to study Scripture and disciple one another. IF:Locals meet in living rooms, churches, and even prisons.

Do I need to be part of a church to attend?

No — first-timers are welcome in person or online, whether or not you're already part of a church.

Can men and children be part of IF?

IF:Gathering itself is built for women, but the wider IF family also welcomes men and children into the larger Gather movement.

I've never been to an IF:Gathering before — is it for me?

Yes. Most of what happens at IF starts with someone showing up for the first time, not knowing anyone. That's exactly who this is for.
