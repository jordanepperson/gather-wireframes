Our story

What started as a gathering for women is now a global movement of people gathering for the sake of the gospel. Twelve years in, IF:Gathering remains discipleship-first — life-on-life, in living rooms, in local churches, studying God's Word together.

What IF:Gathering is

IF:Gathering empowers women to disciple in their own context — making disciples who make disciples, wherever they are. The broader IF family also draws men and children into the wider Gather ecosystem.

FAQ

Why is IF:Gathering two days again?

We're returning to the original two-day format for 2027, after 2026's one-night event — more room for teaching, worship, and community.

What is an IF:Local?

A small group — as few as 5 people — gathering year-round to study Scripture and disciple one another.

Do I need to be part of a church to attend?

No — first-timers are welcome in person or online.
