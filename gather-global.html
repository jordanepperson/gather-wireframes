FAQ

What does hosting a watch party require?

A space, a screen, and a few volunteers — we provide the broadcast, a host guide, and promotional materials.

How long is the broadcast?

24 hours, relayed across regional hubs so every time zone has a moment built for them.

Is there a cost to host?

No — hosting a watch party is free. Optional add-ons are available for larger gatherings.

What happens after the broadcast?

You'll receive a simple next-step guide for your specific gathering. Details in your host toolkit.

Who is Gather27 for?

Gather27 is for every man, woman, and child on Earth who follows Jesus — every denomination, every continent, every generation. There's no ticket, application, or membership required.

What happens during the 24 hours?

Through live-streaming technology, the global Church gathers for prayer, worship, repentance, and commissioning. Regional hubs each host a session in turn, relaying the broadcast around the world as it follows the sun.

Where are the Gather27 broadcast sites?

Regional hubs currently include Dallas (North America), Auckland (Oceania), Kuala Lumpur (Asia), Cluj (Eastern Europe), Kigali (Africa), London (Western Europe), and Lima (South America), alongside undisclosed sites serving the persecuted Church.

What is a Gather Group?

A Gather Group is a local gathering — in a church, a living room, on a campus, anywhere — organized by a host to bring their community into the broadcast together. Gather25 saw over 21,000 registered worldwide.

Who are the partners behind Gather27?

A growing list of global Christian organizations — including IF:Gathering, YouVersion, Cru, Alpha, and dozens more — are collaborating to make Gather27 possible. See the full list in the Partners section above.

When is Gather27?

Gather27 is a 24-hour global broadcast taking place March 5–6, 2027, moving continuously from region to region as it follows the sun across time zones.

Is Gather27 free to watch?

Yes. Gather27 is completely free, everywhere — the broadcast, host guides, and promotional materials all come at no cost.

How is Gather27 different from Gather25?

Gather25 was the first 25-hour global broadcast, held in March 2025. Gather27 is the next iteration two years later — same spirit of prayer, worship, repentance, and commissioning, expanded to reach even more of the global Church.

How do I watch Gather27 online?

The broadcast streams free from this site and through partner platforms; register to host a watch party to get early access to streaming details as they're announced.

Pray

What We're Praying For

Across every regional hub, the same requests keep surfacing. Wherever you're watching from, you're invited to pray these with us:

  • Revival among the next generation, as Gen Z steps into leading what God is doing.
  • Protection and boldness for the persecuted Church, wherever the cost of following Jesus is highest.
  • Unity across denominations, cultures, and continents, one undivided Church.
  • Laborers for the harvest, more people willing to go, teach, and translate.
  • Reconciliation, in homes, in nations, and between the Church and the communities it has failed.

Repent

Why We Repent Together

Gather27 sets aside time for the global Church to repent together. This is a shared posture, not a public confession of individual sin. We're asking God to search our hearts as one body, to show us where we've grown comfortable, divided, or silent, and to renew us for what He's calling us to next.

Repentance connects our prayer and worship to action. It's how we prepare to be sent.
