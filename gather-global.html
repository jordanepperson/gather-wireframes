FAQ

Who is Gather27 for?

Gather27 is for every man, woman, and child on Earth who follows Jesus, from every denomination, every continent, every generation. There's no ticket, application, or membership required.

When is Gather27?

Gather27 is a 24-hour global broadcast taking place March 5 to 6, 2027, moving continuously from region to region as it follows the sun across time zones.

How long is the broadcast, and what happens during it?

Gather27 runs 24 hours, relayed across regional hubs so every time zone gets a moment built for them. The global Church gathers for prayer, worship, repentance, and commissioning, with each hub hosting a session in turn as the broadcast follows the sun.

Is Gather27 free to watch?

Yes. Gather27 is completely free, everywhere. The broadcast, host guides, and promotional materials all come at no cost.

How do I watch Gather27 online?

The broadcast streams free from this site and through partner platforms. Register to host a gathering to get early access to streaming details as they're announced.

What does hosting a gathering require?

A space, a screen, and a few volunteers. We provide the broadcast, a host guide, and promotional materials.

Is there a cost to host?

No. Hosting a gathering is free. Optional add-ons are available for larger gatherings.

What is a gathering?

A gathering is a local group — in a church, a living room, on a campus, anywhere — organized by a host to bring their community into the broadcast together. Gather25 saw over 21,000 registered worldwide.

What happens after the broadcast?

You'll receive a simple next-step guide for your specific gathering. Details are in your host toolkit.

This Is the Moment.

This gathering happens in churches, schools, living rooms, and coffee shops through personal invites, not big ad campaigns. Be part of the Church gathering as one.

What We're Praying For

Across every regional hub, the same requests keep surfacing. Wherever you're watching from, you're invited to pray these with us:

  • Revival among the next generation, as Gen Z steps into leading what God is doing.
  • Protection and boldness for the persecuted Church, wherever the cost of following Jesus is highest.
  • Unity across denominations, cultures, and continents, one undivided Church.
  • Laborers for the harvest, more people willing to go, teach, and translate.
  • Reconciliation, in homes, in nations, and between the Church and the communities it has failed.

Repent

Why We Repent Together

Gather27 sets aside time for the global Church to repent together. This is a shared posture, not a public confession of individual sin. We're asking God to search our hearts as one body, to show us where we've grown comfortable, divided, or silent, and to renew us for what He's calling us to next.

Repentance connects our prayer and worship to action. It's how we prepare to be sent.
