Gather With Us

A Global Movement

For the 5.5 Billion

There are 5.5 billion people who don't yet know Jesus. For the first time in history, believers everywhere can gather, pray, and mobilize together in real time — across every continent, every time zone. Hearts are turning toward God in homes, cities, and nations. The Church was not meant to stand still in the middle of it.

A Movement Founded by Jennie Allen

If Jesus came back tomorrow, then what?

In 2014, Jennie Allen and a small team began gathering people to live like the answer matters. What grew from it became Gather. In 2025, our first global broadcast brought together more than 7 million people across 225 countries to pray, worship, and respond — and it was only the beginning. Gather continues to create moments where the global Church sees what God is doing, celebrates it, and joins in.
