Happening Around the World

1/4
DatesEvery Tuesday, 8 AM CST
DetailsVirtual

Each week, believers from around the world gather to pray together. In a spirit of humility, we come before God to repent, worship, and be renewed by His presence. This call has become a steady rhythm for thousands — a place to bring the weight of what's happening in your city, your family, and the nations, and lay it down together. There's no commitment beyond showing up; just come as you are and pray.

DatesMarch 5-6, 2027
DetailsLive BroadcastIn-Person

Gather27 is a 24-hour global broadcast event designed to bring Christians together across all continents, time zones, and denominations through worship, prayer, and repentance. Hosted from a rotating slate of cities, the broadcast moves with the sun, handing off from region to region so every time zone gets its own moment in the spotlight. Churches, campuses, and living rooms around the world will tune in together, making it one of the largest single days of collective prayer in the movement's history.

3/4
DatesSeptember 12, 2026
DetailsIn-PersonDallas, TX

Local churches across Dallas are gathering for a night of worship, prayer, and vision for what God is doing in our city — a taste of the momentum building toward Gather27. Pastors from a dozen congregations are co-hosting, sharing a single stage and a single microphone, which is itself part of the point: this isn't any one church's event, it's the whole city's. Expect worship, short testimonies from local ministries, and a clear next step for getting involved beyond the night itself.

DatesOctober 1-2, 2027

IF:Gathering exists to gather, equip, and unleash women to live on mission. Across cities, churches, campuses, and living rooms, women come together to worship, learn from Scripture, and be reminded of their identity in Christ. What started as a single weekend gathering has grown into a year-round network of small groups — IF:Local tables — meeting in homes and churches everywhere, discipling each other long after the broadcast ends.

A Movement Founded by Jennie Allen

If Jesus came back tomorrow, then what?

In 2014, Jennie Allen and a small team began gathering people to live like the answer matters. In 2025, that grew into Gather's first global broadcast, reaching 7 million people across 225 countries. It was only the beginning.
