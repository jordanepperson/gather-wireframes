Why now

There are 5.5 billion people who don't yet know Jesus. For the first time in history, believers everywhere can gather, pray, and mobilize together in real time — across every continent, every time zone. Hearts are turning toward God in homes, cities, and nations. The Church was not meant to stand still in the middle of it.

What Gather does

Gather convenes; our partners equip and send. We don't replace the local church or discipleship organizations — we connect them.

It started with a question

It started with a question Jennie Allen couldn't shake: if Jesus came back tomorrow, then what? In 2014, she and a small team began gathering people to live like the answer matters. Jennie is Gather's Chief Storyteller — the reach engine for this movement, not its destination.

Stoking revival fires that reawaken the global Church

In 2025, our first global broadcast brought together more than 7 million people across 225 countries and territories to pray, worship, and respond. It was only the beginning.

USA — a campus watch party turned into a weekly prayer group.

Rwanda — churches gathered by the hundreds to broadcast together.

Ukraine — believers prayed together across active conflict zones.

Malaysia — a living room gathering grew into a citywide network.
